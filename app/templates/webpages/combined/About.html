About Our Project!


 We created this project to provide a convenient way to write new stories for children! Reading is beneficial in many ways, but kids get bored of reading the same books over and over again. With our model, your child can enjoy a brand new, interesting story every night. Using the program is quick and easy, and your child can even use it on their own! Simply think of a prompt, enter it into our program, and receive an original story in no time!

How To Use

Follow these simple steps to write your own children's story!

Step 1

Think of a prompt that you want to begin your story with. Make sure that it is about one sentence long.

Step 2

Enter your prompt into the text box by clicking the 'Start' button, speaking your prompt, and clicking the 'Stop' button.

Step 3

Click the 'Generate' button, wait a few seconds, and read your new story that was generated by the program!
